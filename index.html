places you must visit

Disney World
It was the best place ever!

I went to the Disney World in Florida. It was awesome! I went to all four main Disney lands on different days and years. First, I went to Magic Kingdom. There were fantastic fireworks at the end! Then, I went to Epcot. I went on mini boat rides and my favorite ride was Soaring! It was so cool. You feel like your soaring in the sky, but you are just sitting and basically watching TV. Next, I went to Animal Kingdom. The Everest ride was the best! It was going up and down Mt. Everest, but there were train tracks that were "broken" and "Yeti's". There was a part where we went backwards. Last, I went to Hollywood Studios. There was an epic fall on the Tower of Terror! Overall, Disney World was amazing.

Myrtle Beach
Best Beach Ever!

I went to Myrtle Beach in South Carolina with my friend. I went there in my car with my friend. We were playing word games and listening to music. As we were nearing our hotel, we were yelling and singing in the car. In the hotel, we were playing a very random game with the suitcase trolley. The next day, we went to the hotel swimming pool and went down a mega slide! We went to the beach and sat by the water. It was very fun. After that, we washed off and went to downtown to go on a ferris wheel ride! We could see the ocean from high in the sky. We went to the pier and enjoyed great sea food. That was the end of the day.

review on the movies

Mulan: Live-Action
Excellent movie for sleepover

I saw with my friends at a sleepover. It was really fun and nice, except for how two of them kept talking. The movie was very good. There was no romance and drama. At least the romance only went till holding hands. It is a very appropriate movie for kids.

Frozen 2
Superb movie

I saw it in the theater with my friends(again) and I really liked it (except for the friends part). Back to the movie part. I liked how it was very adventurous and was very much a sequel of Frozen 1. It finished all the mysteries that weren't covered in part 1. Overall, it was a very good movie to watch with small kids.

Aladdin
Superb movie you can actually watch with friends

I saw the movie in the theater with my friends(again) and I liked it. I liked all the songs in the movie and how it was very peaceful(ish). It was very not so sad. I liked Abu the most because he was the most playful monkey I have ever seen.

Coco

  • Hasbro, Mattel Say Toy Boom to Extend Beyond Covid-19

    U.S. toy sales jumped in 2020 as parents diverted spending to Monopoly and Barbie dolls

    Hasbro Inc. and Mattel Inc. enjoyed a boost during the Covid-19 pandemic as lockdowns spurred parents to spend on everything from board games to Barbie dolls for their children. Now the toy makers are trying to determine whether new habits such as game night and playtime will remain once the pandemic subsides. They join others, from the makers of Purell hand sanitizer to Peloton bikes, in assessing their businesses. Widespread lockdowns and a lack of social interaction boosted U.S. toy sales 16% last year to more than $25 billion, according to research firm NPD Group Inc. Parents diverted spending on canceled vacations and summer camps to in-home diversions. Stimulus payments helped the industry too.
